If you are using vagrant and you want to shutdown your virtual environment,
follow the instructions below.

### Stopping your virtual environment

1. Disconnect from your virtual environment by typing `logout` at the vagrant
prompt like this:

```text
vagrant@precise32:~$ logout
Connection to 127.0.0.1 closed.
your_name: rbb$ 
```

2. Then stop your virtual environment by typing `vagrant halt` at your computer's
command prompt like this:

```text
your_name: rbb$ vagrant halt
[default] Attempting graceful shutdown of VM...
your_name: rbb$ 
```

3. You can now exit your terminal or command prompt session by typing `exit`.
